What We Do


What.. This website is designed to connect you to someone who has a similar interest. Choose from a variety of activities, including, Running, Biking, Swimming, Golfing, Skiing, and Snowboarding.


How.. We ask that you give some general information, such as, your age, gender and location. Then you will input the activity you enjoy participating in along with some additional information about it. Last, you let us know what kind of person you are hoping to connect with.


Where.. This service is available to anyone in the United States.


Why.. Our hope is to encourage everybody to get out and be active. It is difficult to go out alone, this is a way to connect with someone who is interested in the same thing. It is a way to make a new friend who you can continue doing this activity with.
