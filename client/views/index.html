What We Do


What... This website is designed to connect you to someone who has a similar interest. Choose from a variety of activities, including, Running, Biking, Swimming, Golfing, Skiing, and Snowboarding. This is for peole who need someone to who can give them the motivation to get out and get active.


How... We ask that you give some general information, such as, your age, gender and location. Then you will input the activity you enjoy participating in along with some additional information about it. Last, you let us know what kind of person you are hoping to connect with. Then it will send you 3 recommended partners who might be a good fit for you.


Where... This service is available to anyone in the United States. With some help, we would like to make this available to everyone. Our home base, as of now, is in Colorado.


Why... Our hope is to encourage everybody to get out and be active. It is difficult to go out alone, this is a way to connect with someone who is interested in the same thing. It is a way to make a new friend who you can continue doing this activity with.

Overview

Thank you for signing up with Active Allies. You are on your way to finding the perfect partner for your favorite activity. On the left, use the sidebar to navigate through your information.

Click on the Edit Profile tab to change any of the original information you submitted. That is also where you can update your username and password.

The Edit Activity tab is where you can submit the activity you are interested in participating in. You will also be required to input a little information about the activity you chose. With your activity choice submitted, now you will be recommended to others as a possible partner.

When you are ready to find a partner, click on the Partner Info tab. This is where you will input specific information about a potential partner. Please answer every questions, so your recommendation can be as accurate as possible. When you submit the form, up to 3 profiles will appear on the screen. If you are interesting in talking to any of them, click on the green check at the bottom of the profile. This will send an invitation to join a private chat room with you. If they accept the invitation, they will send you a message. Then you can start a conversation finding out where you can meet, or gather personal information from each other.

The chat room is found in the Chat Room tab. After you send the invitation, click this tab to send them messages. Their profile should appear to the side of the chat.

Click Find a Trail to search for trails or other points of interests. Google Maps is provided there, with a search function.

For answers to any questions you may have, please click the Help tab. If you don't find the answer you were looking for, please send us an email.
